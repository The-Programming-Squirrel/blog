About Us

Welcome to The-Programming-Squirrel, your trusted partner in navigating the exciting world of cybersecurity! Founded by Nyki Anderson, our mission is to empower beginners and women aspiring to break into the cybersecurity industry. We understand the unique challenges faced by newcomers, especially women, in this dynamic field. That’s why we provide comprehensive certification study guides and educational materials designed to simplify complex concepts, build confidence, and set you up for success in your cybersecurity journey.

At The-Programming-Squirrel, we believe that advocacy and outreach can inspire a new generation of women to pursue rewarding careers in cybersecurity. Through relatable content, practical resources, and a supportive community, we aim to create a welcoming space where everyone—regardless of background—feels encouraged to learn, grow, and thrive. Join us as we work to make cybersecurity accessible, inclusive, and empowering for all.
